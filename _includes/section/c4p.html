Call for papers

BSidesPEI is considering having 2 tracks throughout the day. Track 1 is open to anyone, and track 2 will be reserved for first time presenters. We want the event to be a safe fun place for people to engage with the security community and to improve their skills.

Submissions should be emailed to cfp@bsidespei.ca

The following information should be submitted at minimum:

  • Name and contact information (email, phone, LinkedIn, Twitter)
  • Title of Presentation
  • Abstract of your presentation limited to 200 words or less for use on the B-Sides PEI website
  • Speaker Bio for the B-Sides PEI website
  • Full description of presentation (if your abstract exceeds 200 words)
  • List of presentation requirements, besides the following: power, projector with VGA input, sound, and internet connectivity

Successful speakers will be notified via email as papers are received and selected. Get your papers in soon!

  • NOW - CFP open
  • July 20th, 2016 - CFP closed

If you have questions please email cfp@bsidespei.ca
